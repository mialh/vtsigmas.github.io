Recruitment Schedule

The SYZters of Sigma Psi Zeta at Virginia Tech would like to formally announce the Spring 2023 Recruitment Schedule: The SYZn is Upon Us! If you have questions, please reach out to our recruitment chairs, Esha Islam (enislam@vt.edu) and Crystal Lee (crystalslee23@vt.edu)!






A B O U T

Sigma Psi Zeta is a multicultural, Asian-interest (but not Asian-exclusive!) sorority. We value unity, strength, professionalism and sisterhood. Our National Philanthropy is to Combat the Violence Against Women in its varied forms. Including, but not limited to, domestic violence and sex trafficking. We are an official partner of the White House initiative, It's On Us, a campaign to end sexual assault on college campuses. We do not believe there is a typical Sigma; Sisterhood is not a mold for women to fit into. In fact, our differences are what enhance and enrich our experiences as sisters. If you are looking for a group of lifelong friends who will encourage you to be the best you can be, come out and see if we are the sisterhood for you.


R E C R U I T M E N T

Recruitment week allows attendees to familiarize themselves with the sisters and fundamental beliefs of our sorority. Recruitment events are FREE and there is NO obligation to join, so any interested students are welcome to come out to our events. We host a variety of events throughout the week (from social to educational) to help potential new members determine their perfect fit.

To be eligible for a bid, you must attend at least 1 general interest meeting (GIM) and 2 other events.
